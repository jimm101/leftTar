Understanding Scale Positions

Each visualization represents a different approach to learning the A Major scale on guitar. Traditional methods use "box patterns" confined to 4-5 frets, while modern approaches like 3-notes-per-string prioritize speed and consistency. The CAGED system uses familiar chord shapes, diagonal patterns emphasize melodic flow, and harmonic zones reveal the theoretical relationships between notes. Try combining these approaches to master the entire fretboard!
